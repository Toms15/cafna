When a Neapolitan is happy for whatever reason, instead of paying for just one coffee, the one he’s drinking, he’ll pay for two: one for himself and one for the next customer. It’s like taking the rest of the world out for a coffee…

01

Real neapolitan espresso

Premium coffee, made to be special.

The authentic flavour is achieved by hand-roasting Arabica coffee beans, taking care over the quality and blend in order to draw out the coffee’s freshness and intense aroma.

Find out more
02

In an authentic neapolitan home

Enjoying a “cup of coffee” as though you were in an authentic Neapolitan home: that’s how our interiors are designed to make you feel.

The traditional majolica tile flooring, sea-blue walls and the street-front counter are the defining elements of our Neapolitan living room, in which having a coffee is nothing short of a ritual.

Find out more
03

Wherever and whenever you want

Caffè Napoli is an international chain with a contemporary feel. It’s a place where you can discover and savour an excellent espresso – more precisely, the quintessential Neapolitan espresso that’s celebrated the world over for its superlative taste. Sip it in a pleasant, stylish environment for a unique experience: a treat for the eyes and the taste buds.
